About Hoài Đặng

Hoài Đặng (Hoai Dang) is a Vietnamese content creator and entrepreneur living in Odessa, Texas, United States.

He shares real stories about life in America, business experiences, and the everyday journey of Vietnamese immigrants in the United States.

Hoài is also the owner of Annie's Nails & Spa, a nail salon located in Odessa, Texas, serving the local community.

Through platforms like TikTok, Facebook, and YouTube, Hoài shares insights about working, entrepreneurship, and life in Texas.
